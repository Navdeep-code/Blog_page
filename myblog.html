Bucket List Travel: Dubai

  1. Burj Khalifa

    Dubai is incomplete without the burj khalifa that dominates the skycraper-filled skyline.This 830m tall tower attracts billions of tourists each year.The world's fastest elevator takes the tourist to view the different levels of the building.On 125th floor the photographs of the visitors are taken and are made available to them for purchase as souvenirs.The foot of the tower is really popular among tourists due to their dancing fountain shows.Music is played on the Burj Khalifa observation decks for these.

  2. Museum of the Future

    Catch the glimpse of the future by visiting this unique masterpiece.This Museum with its architecture is spellbounding and engages very person who walks through its doors in the world of science and technology.It is a place of learning, where people explore the challenges and opportunities shaping our times, and finding solutions for a better future.

    Nature it self is a miracle and, it has it has its own power known as flowers, mountains, sky,ocean and greenery. Nature has power to heal a lot of things. One of its power is flowers. In this place widely known as Miracle Garden, which is situated in Dubailand, Dubai, UAE. This garden provides wide range of flowers to see. The scent of flowes are so refreshing that it fills the air with pure freshness. It also has sculpters of disney characters which fills heart of people with joy.
